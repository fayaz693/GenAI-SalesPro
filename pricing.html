Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a free trial?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial on all plans. No credit card required. You get full access to all features during the trial period.
Can I change plans later?
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and wire transfers for Enterprise plans.
Is there a contract or can I cancel anytime?
No long-term contracts required! You can cancel your subscription at any time. Monthly plans can be cancelled with immediate effect, while annual plans are refunded on a pro-rata basis.
Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?
Yes! We offer special discounts for nonprofit organizations and educational institutions. Contact our sales team for more information.
What kind of support do you provide?
All plans include email support. Professional plans get priority support with faster response times. Enterprise customers get a dedicated account manager and 24/7 phone support.
